About Us

DoorTask interior carpentry services offer a wide range of woodwork service in a faster and safer way, Our trained in-house carpenters provide you with the installation of all carpentry related works. That includes doors, wooden cots, window frames, furniture works, shelving, bookcase, broken doorknobs, hinges, handles and a lot more.

Our carpenters will come to your doorstep at a specified time that is convenient for you. DoorTask's sole aim is customer satisfaction by providing quality service at an affordable price.

Our skilled carpenters can undertake:

  • Wardrobes;
  • Bookcases and Library Furniture;
  • Floating Shelves;
  • Storage Cabinets;
  • Cupboards;
  • Tables;
  • Chairs & Doors etc.;
